Steve Jobs

  • Posted on, July 19, 2020

Guys, today I'm going to talk about a man we all know very well. Which revolutionized the electronics and computer industry. The man is none other than Steve Jobs, founder of the world's first trillion-dollar company, Apple Inc.

Steve Paul Jobs was a Great leader, thinker, innovator, industrial designer and entrepreneur, born February 24, 1955 in San Francisco, California, United States. Steve was from a middle class family. So, his parents had to work hard to give him good education and meet all his needs, even though they could not pay the college fees, so Steve dropped out of college.

He then built the Macintosh operating system with his college friend Steve Wozniak in his father's garage. In 1976, at the young age of 20, Steve started a company called Apple Inc. Within a few years, Steve's hard work turned the company into a $2 billion company with 4,000 employees.

“Being the richest man in the cemetery doesn't matter to me. Going to bed at night saying we've done something wondurful, that's what matters.”
-- Steve Jobs

Proved that Apple buyers believe in "Think different". This was Apple's famous ad. As it turns out, this is about people who are different . Even if they are look good or bad, but we can't ignore them, because they are world changers.

Steve had been suffering from a disease called pancreatic cancer for a long time. He also underwent many surgeries. However, Steve Jobs died of pancreatic cancer on October 5, 2011 in Palo Alto, California, United States. But to this day, his popularity remains the same as before.
