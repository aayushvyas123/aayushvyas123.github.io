Sushant Singh Rajput

  • Posted on, July 31, 2020

The person I am going to talk about today was not the son of a billionaire or a big Bollywood actor, but his skills, hard work and talent made him very popular and self made actor in Bollywood. That man is none other than our beloved Sushant Singh Rajput. Sushant who got 7th rank in AIEEE , he was very gifted in teaching, though he left engineering and made a career in acting and he reached the pinnacle in acting. So let's see his journey from Bihar to Bollywood Star.

Sushant Singh Rajput was an Indian actor, dancer and entrepreneur. Sushant was born on January 21, 1986 in Patna, Bihar. He was the youngest of five siblings, Being the youngest in the family, he was very dear to the family. He completed elementary education from St. Karen's High School. However, his mother died in 2002 when he was 16 years old. Sushant was closest to his mother. His family then migrated to Delhi. Sushant then completed his higher secondary education from Kulachi Hansraj Model School. He then got admission in Mechanical Engineering at Delhi Technological University. And in addition to studying there, he also joined a dance class and for his good dance performance, he was first selected as a background dancer at the 2006 Seventh International Indian Film Academy Awards (IIFA). As part of the troupe, he was as a backup dancer during Aishwarya Rai's performance at the 2006 Commonwealth Games closing ceremony in Australia.

Unable to focus on his studies, Sushant left in his third year of engineering. Then he came to Mumbai and started learning dance and acting, which was his dream and he also had enthusiasm in it. Shortly afterwards, due to his talent, he got a acting role in a TV serial called "Pavitra Rishta". Shortly afterwards, due to his talent, he got a acting role in a TV serial called "Pavitra Rishta".

While working in this serial, Sushant got awards like Best Male Actor and Most Popular Actor. His superb performance opened up his paths to the Bollywood. However, before entering Bollywood, he also participated in reality shows like Zara Nachke Dikha and Jhalak Dikhla Jaa in 2011. Shortly thereafter, he starred lead role in the film Kai Po Che!. The film proved to be a huge success after its release. And seeing his fabulous work in Kai Po Che, he started getting offers to work in films. There he made superhit blockbuster movies like Shuddh Desi Romance, PK, Raabta, Kedarnath and Chhichhore and reached the peak of success.

On July 14, 2020, Sushant committed suicide at his home in Bandra and shortened his life. However, the exact cause of his death is yet to be ascertained. But Sushant won the hearts of everyone with his good artistry, innocence and good deeds towards the society so his fans cannot forget him. The man who taught us how to live is no longer with us. But his presence is still felt today.
